# Sales GPT

## Purpose

Support sales teams with account research, discovery prep, proposal drafting,
objection handling, CRM note cleanup, and follow-up emails.

## Owner

- Primary owner: Head of Sales.
- Backup owner: Revenue Operations.

## Risk Level

High.

## Permission Level

Level 3 if using account or opportunity data. Level 2 if using only public
sales collateral.

## Approved Data Sources

- Approved sales collateral.
- Product/service descriptions.
- Public prospect information.
- Approved case studies.
- CRM exports approved by the data owner.

## Forbidden Data

- Customer secrets.
- Unapproved pricing exceptions.
- Legal commitments.
- Contract terms not approved by legal.
- Private prospect data not authorized for AI use.

## Audit Requirements

- Monthly sample review.
- Approved collateral review.
- Output review for pricing, promises, and legal commitments.
- Incident route for customer-facing errors.

## Approval Gate

Head of Sales and data owner approval required. Legal review required if the GPT
drafts contract language or binding commitments.

## Review Cadence

Monthly.
