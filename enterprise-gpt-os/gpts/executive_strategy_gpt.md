# Executive Strategy GPT

## Purpose

Support leadership with strategic planning, decision framing, meeting prep,
scenario analysis, and executive synthesis.

## Owner

- Primary owner: Chief of Staff.
- Backup owner: Executive Assistant or Strategy Lead.

## Risk Level

High.

## Permission Level

Level 3 by default. Level 4 if it touches finance, legal, HR, investor,
security, or acquisition-sensitive material.

## Approved Data Sources

- Approved strategy documents.
- Board-ready summaries.
- OKRs.
- Market research approved for leadership use.
- Public competitor research.

## Forbidden Data

- Unapproved financial forecasts.
- Legal privileged material unless specifically approved.
- Personnel files.
- Security-sensitive documents.
- Confidential acquisition material unless executive-approved.

## Audit Requirements

- Monthly output review.
- Prompt and source change log.
- Decision-use disclaimer.
- Executive owner review before major updates.

## Approval Gate

Executive sponsor and data owner approval required.

## Review Cadence

Monthly.
