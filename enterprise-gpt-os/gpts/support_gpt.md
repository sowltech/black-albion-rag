# Support GPT

## Purpose

Help support teams answer customer questions, summarize tickets, draft replies,
and find approved troubleshooting steps.

## Owner

- Primary owner: Head of Support.
- Backup owner: Support Operations.

## Risk Level

High.

## Permission Level

Level 2 for public help center material. Level 3 if customer ticket data is
used. Level 4 if regulated, financial, health, legal, or security-sensitive
customer data is involved.

## Approved Data Sources

- Help center articles.
- Approved troubleshooting guides.
- Product documentation.
- Escalation rules.
- Customer tickets approved for AI processing.

## Forbidden Data

- Passwords.
- API keys.
- Payment card data.
- Sensitive customer secrets.
- Unapproved regulated data.
- Internal-only incident details unless approved.

## Audit Requirements

- Monthly sample output review.
- Escalation accuracy checks.
- Customer-facing response quality checks.
- Incident route for incorrect or unsafe answers.

## Approval Gate

Support owner, data owner, and IT/security approval required. Legal/compliance
review required for regulated support contexts.

## Review Cadence

Monthly.
