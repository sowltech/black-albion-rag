# Company Knowledge GPT

## Purpose

Help staff find approved company knowledge, policies, SOPs, FAQs, and internal
reference material.

## Owner

- Primary owner: Knowledge Manager.
- Backup owner: Operations Lead.

## Risk Level

Medium.

## Permission Level

Level 2: approved internal documents and SOPs.

## Approved Data Sources

- Staff handbook.
- Approved SOP library.
- Internal FAQ.
- Policy documents.
- Training notes approved for staff use.

## Forbidden Data

- Passwords.
- API keys.
- HR case files.
- Legal privileged material.
- Customer secrets.
- Financial records unless explicitly approved.

## Audit Requirements

- Quarterly owner review.
- Sample output checks.
- Data source review.
- Prompt change log.

## Approval Gate

Department lead approval required before rollout.

## Review Cadence

Quarterly.
