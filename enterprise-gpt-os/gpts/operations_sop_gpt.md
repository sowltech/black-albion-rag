# Operations SOP GPT

## Purpose

Help staff follow approved operating procedures, checklists, internal workflows,
and handoff steps.

## Owner

- Primary owner: Operations Lead.
- Backup owner: Process Manager.

## Risk Level

Medium by default. High if outputs affect customer commitments, safety,
payments, compliance, or regulated operations.

## Permission Level

Level 2 by default. Level 3 if confidential operating data is included.

## Approved Data Sources

- Approved SOPs.
- Process maps.
- Checklists.
- Escalation paths.
- Training guides.

## Forbidden Data

- Secrets.
- Credentials.
- Unapproved customer records.
- Legal or HR case material.
- Draft procedures not approved for operational use.

## Audit Requirements

- Quarterly SOP source review.
- Sample workflow output review.
- Change log for SOP updates.
- Incident route for incorrect operational guidance.

## Approval Gate

Operations lead approval required before pilot. High-risk workflows require
department and IT/security review.

## Review Cadence

Quarterly for medium risk. Monthly for high risk.
