## Status Values

- `Requested`
- `In review`
- `Approved`
- `Pilot`
- `Active`
- `Paused`
- `Retired`
- `Rejected`

## Required Fields

- GPT ID.
- Name.
- Department.
- Owner.
- Business purpose.
- Risk level.
- Permission level.
- Data sources.
- Approval status.
- Audit requirement.
- Review cadence.

## Owner Responsibilities

- Keep purpose and scope current.
- Confirm data sources remain approved.
- Review output quality.
- Review access permissions.
- Record material prompt or knowledge changes.
- Escalate incidents.
- Complete review cadence.

## Audit Requirements

- Low risk: owner review log.
- Medium risk: owner review log plus sample output checks.
- High risk: approval record, change log, sample output checks, incident route.
- Critical risk: executive approval, detailed audit log, restricted access,
  formal review before rollout and after changes.
