# 90-Day GPT Rollout Plan

## Purpose

Roll out GPTs in controlled phases so the business can learn, govern, and scale
without losing ownership or auditability.

## Days 1-30: Foundation

### Goals

- Create GPT registry.
- Assign owners.
- Classify existing GPTs.
- Identify high-risk use cases.
- Approve initial low/medium-risk pilots.

### Tasks

- Create registry from `registry/gpt_registry_template.md`.
- Review active GPTs.
- Assign risk and permission levels.
- Complete build templates for priority GPTs.
- Establish audit and incident process.

### Success Metrics

- 100% of known GPTs registered.
- Owners assigned.
- High-risk GPTs identified.
- First pilots approved.

## Days 31-60: Pilot

### Goals

- Pilot approved GPTs with controlled user groups.
- Capture output quality issues.
- Confirm audit requirements.
- Train users on boundaries.

### Tasks

- Run pilot tests.
- Review sample outputs.
- Record prompt and data changes.
- Collect user feedback.
- Fix unsafe or unclear behavior.

### Success Metrics

- Pilot GPTs have review notes.
- No unowned GPTs.
- Incidents logged and handled.
- User guidance delivered.

## Days 61-90: Scale

### Goals

- Roll out approved GPTs.
- Retire unsafe or duplicate GPTs.
- Establish monthly/quarterly review cadence.
- Prepare next wave of GPT requests.

### Tasks

- Approve rollout for successful pilots.
- Update registry status.
- Schedule recurring reviews.
- Archive rejected GPTs.
- Start next request cycle.

### Success Metrics

- Approved GPTs active.
- Risk reviews scheduled.
- Audit evidence stored.
- High-risk GPTs have owner and escalation route.
